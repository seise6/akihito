The Warrior's Temple

Because Akihito is an old man he doesn't move any more than he has to. Despite being old, he is actually very agile and muscular. Because he was a samurai back in his hay day, the Professor is well versed in combat and gives off an intense yet experienced vibe as his presence takes control of a room he is in.

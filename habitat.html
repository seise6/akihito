Akihito Mountain

Professor Akihito exists in the world of bushido where the terrain looks similar to the rural side of Japan and it coexists with some metropolis areas. Both kinds of terrain live in harmony with each other and are filled with people that choose the lifestyles of their respective area. Akihito lives in a rural district by himself up in the mountains.
